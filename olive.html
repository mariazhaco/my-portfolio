Olive Interiors

Olive Interiors was founded with a passion for merging style and sustainability in home decor. Our journey began in response to the growing awareness of environmental impact and the need to make a difference. We believe in creating beauty with a profound sense of responsibility. Our dedication to eco-friendly values drives us to offer sustainable interior solutions crafted from recycled materials. This not only adds a unique charm to your home but also reduces our footprint on the planet.

Design

Stylesheet and designssytem

It all started with research and color mixing until I found a style I wanted to go for. I opted to go for a more minimalistic design with a neutral color palette and simple icons. Olive is not only the name of my product but also one of my favorite colors that I chose to include in this project.

Process

Whireframes & Greybox

The next step in the process was to start sketching my visualisation for this app. I wanted a lot of product pictures and a way to show them all in a inspiring way. All the pitures in the app are AI generated and that was the next step in process, start designing.
